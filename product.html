What is agriculture machines?

Agriculture machines are equipment specifically designed and used for various agricultural tasks such as planting, harvesting, cultivating, and preparing the soil. These machines can be powered by human, animal, or mechanical means, and include various types of equipment such as tractors, plows, cultivators, harvesters, seeders, irrigation systems, and many others. The use of agriculture machines has significantly increased agricultural productivity and efficiency, making it possible for farmers to cultivate more crops in less time with less labor.
